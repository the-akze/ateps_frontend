About our Product

ATEPS is a faster way for attendance for students AND teachers! Our product has an attachable device that can be placed anywhere accessible. To take attendance, simply scan the card at the designated area to sign in. If a student incorrectly scans the device, there is an option to manually change the attendance of someone on our simple and easy to access website. Making use of AI, we were able to eliminate every way of cheating the system. The AI will also detect if there are intruders and immediately lock the door, alerting the admin. Using an Arduino, some components and a Raspberry Pi, we made ATEPS affordable & accessible to everyone!
